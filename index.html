About Us

Future Pediatrics was founded for one reason and one reason only. To help as many children as we can mentally, physically, and emotionally. Almost every member of this practice has children of their own. We understand the struggles of a parent, especially mothers with the stress involved in looking after a child. We are tired of doctors just prescribing medicine. Pediatricians should be there for all aspects of the child, that’s why this pediatric was founded.

To walk hand and hand with the child and his family from birth to adulthood, to ensure their physical and mental well-being.

Meet The Doctors

Dr. Fouad Rasheed

Dr. Fouad Rasheed, MB BCH, has been a doctor for over 47 years. In 1975, he received his medical degree from the Bani Swief Branch of the University of Cairo's Faculty of Medicine.

He dedicates his life to treating children. One particular day, he saw a child 6 hours after calling. After treating the child, he realized that the mother was sicker than the child. This was because no doctor would see her, they were all booked for the week. He had no choice except to examine and treat her immediately.

“Parents Expect us to go beyond with their child, so we always deliver.”

Services
